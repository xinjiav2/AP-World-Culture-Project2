Artifact - 湖南小炒肉

湖南小炒肉 (Hunan Stir-Fried Pork) is a traditional dish from the Hunan province in China. Chinese cuisine is often divided into 8 different regions, and Hunan cuisine specifically is known for bold and spicy flavors, often using chili peppers and garlic. This dish is a great representation of both Hunan cuisine and Chinese cuisine as a whole. Although I am from the Zhejiang province, due to the lack of Zhejiang cuisine within the United States, it is always nice to have a dish that represents Chinese cuisine as a whole.
