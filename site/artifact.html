This page is home to the artifact I have chosen to represent Chinese culture. Presenting: 麻婆豆腐 (Mapo Tofu). A spicy Sichuan dish made with tofu, minced meat (usually pork or beef), and a flavorful sauce made from fermented black beans, chili paste, and Sichuan peppercorns. The dish is known for its bold flavors and numbing spiciness, characteristic of Sichuan cuisine. Mapo Tofu is often served with steamed rice and garnished with chopped green onions. It is a popular comfort food in China and has gained international recognition for its unique taste.
